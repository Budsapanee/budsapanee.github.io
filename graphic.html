artworks to promote features in the LEB2 platform. This platform has been developed to serve professors at the university.

This feature allows professors to develop and create their classes throughout the semester.

Attendance

Let students check-in instead professor call students one-by-one.

Assessment Activity

Let professor add activities for learning and assess them in platform.

Survey

Opportunity for students to assess teaching.

Learning Activity

All materials in the classes will be saved in platform for students.

Online Quiz

Professer can know immediately if student do not understand the lesson.
