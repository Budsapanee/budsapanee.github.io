The Problem

While our customers’ businesses are growing, they would like to know more about their businesses. They would like to know so many information from many angles to help them make the decision and plan their businesses. That’s why the redesign dashboard project was happened.

My Role

For this project, I took on the role of UI designer. My responsibility was to deliver the final design for implementation, which means it had to responsive and support table on a large screen.

Challenge

This was my first job as a UX/UI designer after working as a graphic designer for three years. I had a lot of ground to cover. One of them is to begin talking with users. That user, in particular, is my own CEO. In other words, this includes collaborating closely with the CEO to examine the demands of customers who are actual corporate owner personas.
